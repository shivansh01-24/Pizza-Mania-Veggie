Proudly 100% Vegetarian


At Pizza Mainia, we take ourselves as a sort of veggie-eatery for just the right reason. We know how often a vegan or vegetarian will go out of their way to find that place that really lives up to serving strictly vegetarian food. Several restaurants claim they have vegetarian dishes, but in practice, they don't quite get the idea of not cross-contaminating. We feel proud of our commitment to a safe dining experience by which you can indulge in the absolute freshness of our dishes, free from any kind of concern or further imaginations. This to our entire menu consists of only vegetarian pizzas and burgers, and you can continue indulging in your favorite meals without any worries. We are absolutely vegetarian and proud to be registered under FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India), ensuring the safest and highest hygiene standards of food preparation. Everyone deserves a place where their meals are delectable and of acceptable quality that is high and does not need to be compromised. Your table bears the products offered at Pizza Mainia, carefully selected and developed by our dedicated staff for the variety you enjoy, and rest assured of how every food product was brought with much care and to ensure your health is satisfactory. Let's join in this flavor of safety, a recipe that brings to light beauty within the confines of vegetarian dining, in the mode of joy..
